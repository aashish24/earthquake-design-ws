Design Endpoint Documentation

Request Parameters

Required parameters

latitude
Latitude for location at which to compute composite results.
Example: 34.01
longitude
Longitude for location at which to compute composite results.
Example: -118.01
referenceDocument
Building code reference document identifier.
Example: NEHRP2015
riskCategory
Risk category for structure.
Example: 2
siteClass
NEHRP site classification.
Example: 1
title
Name for site/report.
Example: Test

Output

date
Timestamp
Example: YYYY-MM-DDThh:mm:ssZ
status
String ("success" or "error")
Example: "success"
url
URL to reproduce this result.
Example: {MOUNT_PATH}/design.json?latitude=34.01&longitude=-118.01&referenceDocument=NEHRP2015&riskCategory=2&siteClass=1&title=test
response
Metadata
pgaFloor
Minimum constraint placed on the deterministic ground motion. (Peak Ground Acceleration)
pgaMaxDirection
Factor applyed ot the geometric mean ground motion to get max direction ground motion. (Peak Ground Acceleration)
pgaPercentile
Factor applyed to 50% ground motion to achieve target percentile ground motion. (Peak Ground Acceleration)
s1dFloor
Minimum constraint placed on the deterministic ground motion. (1.0 sec)
s1MaxDirection
Factor applyed ot the geometric mean ground motion to get max direction ground motion. (1.0 sec)
s1Percentile
Factor applyed to 50% ground motion to achieve target percentile ground motion. (.2 sec)
ssdFloor
Minimum constraint placed on the deterministic ground motion. (.2 sec)
ssMaxDirection
Factor applyed ot the geometric mean ground motion to get max direction ground motion. (.2 sec)
ssPercentile
Factor applyed to 50% ground motion to achieve target percentile ground motion. (.2 sec)
Data
designCategory
Seismic design category.
fa
Site amplification factor at 0.2s.
fpga
Site amplification factor at PGA.
fv
Site amplification factor at 1.0s.
pga
Peak Ground Acceleration
pgad
Factor deterministic acceleration value. (Peak Ground Acceleration)
pgam
Site modified peak ground acceleration.
s1
Ground motion with a 2% probability of exceedance in 50 years. (for 1.0s period)
s1d
Factor deterministic acceleration value. (1.0 sec)
s1uh
Mapped uniform-hazard, 5% damped, spectral acceleration.
s1ur
Uniform risk ground motion. (1.0 sec)
sd1
Numeric seismic design value at 1.0 second SA.
sds
Numeric seismic design value at 0.2 second SA.
sdSpectrum
Seismic design spectrum.
sm1
Spectral acceleration value.
sms
Spectral acceleration value.
smSpectrum
Site modified spectrum.
ss
Ground motion with a 2% probability of exceedance in 50 years. (for 0.2s period)
ssd
Factor deterministic acceleration value. (.2 sec)
ssuh
Mapped uniform-hazard, 5% damped, spectral acceleration.
ssur
Uniform risk ground motion. (.2 sec)
tl
Long-period transition period.
